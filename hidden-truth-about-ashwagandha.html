What is ashwagandha, and why does it matter for stress?

Ashwagandha (Withania somnifera) is an adaptogenic herb that's been studied for decades for its effects on the body's stress response — specifically the hypothalamic-pituitary-adrenal (HPA) axis, which controls how much cortisol your body releases under pressure.

PeakModo Sentuel™ uses a standardized root extract delivering 10% withanolides per capsule — withanolides being the steroidal compounds credited with most of ashwagandha's effects on cortisol regulation and stress signaling.
